About Us

Tamakika Savings Investment and credit limited is an investment Society under Tamakika Investments Limited. The society started in the year 2019 and has enjoyed successful growth towards achievement of its mission, vision and its objectives.

Tamakika means “To enlighten” and our main goal is to each other on financial growth opportunities.

This Society runs in form of a Sacco for young people and brings together their savings for investments in high return-low risk investments. In return, the members earn interests and bonus dividends on their savings.

This Society also offers credit facilities in form of loans to its members.

In addition to that, this Society also offers training workshops for members with businesses on how to upgrade their businesses in order to increase on their profit margins.



Our Mission

To enlighten and empower the young generation through creating a savings culture, offering a platform of quick and affordable credit facilities and enabling them exploit every available investment opportunity.

Our Vision

To create a wealth and resourceful young generation by bringing them together through a culture of savings and empowering them towards exploitation of the vast investment opportunities available.

Our Core Values

Integrity; we are trustworthy and act in good faith to execute with the highest degrees of honesty Transparency; we are accountable and clear in all our dealings Commitment; we are dedicated towards individual achievement of every member and the Society in general Reliability; we are dependable and dedicated to our members achievements

How to join:

  • Fully filled registration form
  • Registration fees
  • National ID copy
  • One passport photo

Members are allowed to save any amount below or above the minimum subscription. Every monthly deposit above the minimum monthly subscription will earn interests.

    Members savings earn three types of interests:

  • Per Year Interest (PYI)- At 4% per annum
  • Monthly Accrued Interest (MAI)- At 1% per month
  • Bonus Dividends - Given at the end of the year at a specific percentage of a members total savings.
  • Bonuses– Offered for new member referrals at the end of the year.


Withdrawals

A member is allowed to withdraw funds from his/her account at a charge of 3% or Ksh. 50 whichever is more. The minimum amount that can be withdrawn is Ksh. 1000 and the maximum amount is half the savings balance above the minimum balance of ksh. 5000. Members are only allowed three withdrawals per year.

Termination Of Membership

A member may end his/her contract of membership by submitting a fully filled letter of termination. A termination fee of 10% or sh. 200 whichever is more is charged from his/her nominal savings for that year. The member will receive the remaining savings balance and all interests earned.
