Welcome To Tamakika Services Page

Savings and Dividends

  1. Upon registration the member is required to save a minimum of 400Ksh per month which will earn them returns in terms of dividends
  2. The savings will also act as the primary insurance for when the member is in need of a loan

We offer various types of loans for different circumstances

  1. BizPlus LOAN
  2. JointBiz LOAN
  3. SOKOPLUS LOAN
  4. SME LOAN
  5. Emergency LOAN

Guarantors And security

  1. All guarantors must be memebers of the sacco.
  2. Guarantor should not have a loan balance exceeding two thirds of their total savings.
  3. Guarantor should be fully aware of the total amount used and the category of the loan.
  4. No Member will guaranee more than three loans at a time before previous repayments are done

Requirements For Lending


Lending procedure

  1. Member hands in the filled loan form with other requirements to the Credit Commitee
  3. The Credit commitee holds a special meeting and screens the form by going through the form and other requirements
    4. After the Commitee has come to an agreement they sign the form to confirm their approval
  4. They then hand over the form to the treasurer who communicas with the executive chairman and CEO.
  5. The three executive officials hold a meeting and discuss the report of the credit commitee and authorize the application for disbursement of the loan
  6. The form is handed back to the credit committee for filling
  7. The approval process takes a minimum of three working days

Basic requirements

  1. Applicant must have attained atleast 18 years of age
  2. Must have saved for more than three consecutive months
  3. Fully filled loan application form
  4. Copies of national id
  5. passport photo
  6. Guarantors lega security items

Loan Repayment

The Loanee will be required to pay the loan in full at the end of the of the applied period of repayment. However the credit commitee may offer a grace period The Loanee will be required to service his/her loan in monthly installments.
Only members will have the benefit of reducing balance on their loans of more than two months

Loan Recovery

Should the applicant fail to service the outstanding amount within the specified time of grace period, the amount withstanding shal be recovered from the applicants(member) and guarantor's savings.
The sacco shall have the power to hold the Item of security in its own ownership and wil have the power to recover and resell it or reuse it as their own.

Loan Recovery Process

  • The process of realizing the security shall depend on its nature; howerver the following shall form the general procedures
  • The defaulter shall be given three demand notices through his last known address
  • The guarantor shall be given copies of the demand notices to inform them on the non-repayment by the borrower
  • The society shall refer the matter to the society Lawyer in writing for necessary legal action and subsequent sell of the securities
  • The proceeds realized from the sale of security shall liquidate the outstanding loans plus intereest and any incidental cost
  • Where the sale proceeds does not cover the loan outstanding , the guarantor must be sought to honour their guaranee and pay

Grounds of Loan Rejection

The Credit committee will have power to decline a loan applicaion without giving reason.
The following may lead to rejection of a loan application

  • Loan Purpose
  • Inadequate supporting documents
  • Too many Loans
  • Bad CRB status
  • Inadequate guarantors and security
  • Fake documents(Criminal offence)
  • Wrong Information
  • Excessive guarantee by one member

    • And Other grounds which the credit commitee may consider appropriate for Rejection
